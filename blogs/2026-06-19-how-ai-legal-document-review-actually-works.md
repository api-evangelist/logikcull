AI legal document review is not a black box; it is a human-supervised process where AI speeds up sorting and prioritizing while lawyers still make the final relevance and privilege calls. The article outlines a seven-step workflow: ingesting and processing data, scoping the document set, applying AI categorization, flagging privileged content and personal information, answering questions about documents, conducting human quality control, and producing final redacted materials.
